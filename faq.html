Frequently Asked Questions

Quick, useful answers for planning a visit, choosing a beer, checking offers, and reserving a table at Flow.

What are Flow's timings?

Flow is listed as open from 12 PM to 1 AM. Timings can vary for private events and special nights.

What are the happy hours?

1+1 on all craft beers Monday to Sunday, 12 PM - 7 PM. Late night Happy Hours run Monday to Thursday, 11 PM till closing.

Can I reserve a table?

Yes. Use the website reservation form or call Flow directly for same-day booking confirmation.

Does Flow serve craft beer?

Yes. Flow brews onsite and serves house pours including Octopus Prime Belgian Wit, Captain Bubbles German Wheat, Mangorama Mango Lager, Flowy Joey Munich Helles, NEIPA New England India Pale Ale, Knight Rider Oatmeal Stout, Champagne Like Cider, and Bee Pop Honey Mead.

Are vegetarian options available?

Yes. The menu includes paneer tikka, baby potatoes, baos, salads, pizzas and vegetarian easy plates.

Are aggregator discounts available?

Aggregator offers may vary across platforms such as Zomato, Swiggy Dineout, Magicpin and EazyDiner. Check live offers before booking or payment.
